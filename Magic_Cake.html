Magic Cake

Magi cake is one of the tasties cakes the world as ever known. Few have tried it an lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  • Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  • Spread into a non-stick dish.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  • Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  • Eat and enjoy!
